About Us

Smart Track: Over 30 Years of Pioneering Innovation

For more than three decades, Smart Track has been at the forefront of delivering cutting-edge solutions in Smart Track, Cloud Server, Time Attendance, Personal Tracking, Asset Tracking, Cloud Time Attendance Service, and Time Attendance Terminals. Founded in [1995], our unwavering mission has been to empower businesses with innovative, efficient, and reliable technology that enhances productivity, reduces operational costs, and ensures optimal security.

Our Enduring Legacy

Smart Track’s journey began with a simple yet powerful vision: to provide businesses with a comprehensive suite of tools for effectively managing their most valuable resources – their workforce, their assets, and their data. Throughout our 30+ year history, we’ve remained steadfast in our commitment to anticipating and exceeding the evolving needs of our clients. From pioneering early time attendance systems to developing state-of-the-art cloud-based solutions, Smart Track has consistently led the way in transforming how organizations operate.

  • Smart Track emerged as a leader in traditional time attendance solutions, establishing a reputation for reliability and accuracy. We introduced our first generation of time clocks and software, laying the foundation for future innovations.
  • We expanded our product line to include personal tracking devices and asset tracking solutions, enabling businesses to monitor and manage their mobile workforce and valuable assets with greater efficiency.
  • Smart Track embraced the power of cloud computing, launching our Cloud Time Attendance Service, which revolutionized time and attendance management by offering enhanced accessibility, scalability, and security. We continue to innovate with advanced Time Attendance Terminals incorporating biometric and facial recognition technology.
